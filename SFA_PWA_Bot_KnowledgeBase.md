# SFA PWA Bot Knowledge Base

## Club Membership
- San Fairy Ann Cycling Club (SFACC) is Kent’s largest and friendliest cycling club.
- Membership benefits include access to club rides, events, discounts, curated routes, and more.
- To join, visit: https://www.sanfairyanncc.co.uk/join-the-club

## RideWithGPS Club Account
- SFACC has a club account with RideWithGPS, providing curated cycling routes for all riders in Kent.
- Members can access special features and routes via the club’s RideWithGPS organization page: https://ridewithgps.com/organizations/633-san-fairy-ann-cycling-club
- Benefits:
  - Access to club-curated routes
  - Turn-by-turn navigation and printable cue sheets
  - Community features for sharing rides and connecting with other members
- How to use:
  1. Visit the club’s RideWithGPS organization page
  2. Log in or create a free RideWithGPS account
  3. Request to join the SFACC organization (if required)
  4. Browse and select routes curated by the club
  5. Download routes or use the RideWithGPS app for navigation
- For more info: https://www.sanfairyanncc.co.uk/curated-routes

## Strava: How to Use
- Strava is a social network for athletes, popular for tracking cycling and running activities.
- To use Strava:
  1. Download the Strava app or visit https://www.strava.com/
  2. Create a free account
  3. Record your rides using your phone or GPS device
  4. Join the SFACC club on Strava to connect with other members
  5. Explore and share routes, view leaderboards, and participate in club challenges

## RideWithGPS: How to Use
- RideWithGPS is a platform for planning, sharing, and navigating cycling routes.
- To use RideWithGPS:
  1. Visit https://ridewithgps.com/
  2. Create a free account
  3. Search for routes or create your own
  4. Download routes to your device or use the mobile app for navigation
  5. Join the SFACC organization for access to club routes
